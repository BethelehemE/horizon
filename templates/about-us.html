Horizon

Meet the team!

Horizon, an interactive website focused on providing users with new suggestions on interests, was created as a project by Asia, Bethelehem, Zachary, and Isabella as their final project as part of Google's Computer Science Summer Institute. Horizon is a platform for people around the world to share their interests with other people and discover some new interests.

If you have any questions or would like more information about Horizon and its founders, please contact us at horizon@gmail.com.
