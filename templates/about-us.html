Horizon

Meet the team!

Horizon, an interactive website focused on providing users with new suggestions on interests, was created as a project by Asia, Bethelehem, Zachary, and Isabella as their final project as part of Google's Computer Science Summer Institute. Horizon is a platform for activists around the world to share their opinions on world issues and to make a change.

If you have any questions or would just like more information about Horizon and its founders, please contact us at horizono@gmail.com.
