Horizon

Meet the team!

Horizon, an interactive website focused on providing users with new suggestions on interests, was created as a project by Asia Collins, Bethelehem Engeda, Zachary Rideaux, and Isabella Siu as their final project as part of Google's Computer Science Summer Institute. Horizon is a platform for people around the world to share their interests with other people and discover some new interests.
